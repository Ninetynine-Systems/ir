# Key Texts

## Source anchors

- Surah Al-Baqarah 2:177
- Surah Al-Qamar 54:49
- Sahih Muslim 8

Surah Al-Baqarah 2:177 broadens the picture of righteousness. It ties belief in Allah, the Last Day, the angels, the Book, and the prophets to worship, generosity, promise-keeping, and patience. Faith in the Quran is not merely claimed. It is meant to order the whole person.

Surah Al-Qamar 54:49 adds one of the foundations that Hadith Jibril also names plainly: all things are created with decree. At the foundations level, a believer accepts that Allah knows, wills, and creates with perfect wisdom, even when the details of that wisdom remain hidden from us.

These texts give a stable outline: iman includes revealed beliefs about the unseen, and those beliefs are meant to produce surrender, trust, and obedience.
