# Foundation

## Source anchors

- Surah Al-Baqarah 2:2-5
- Surah Aal-E-Imran 3:102

Taqwa is the believer's carefulness before Allah. It is not mere fear stripped of love and hope, and it is not vague spirituality without obedience. At the foundation level, taqwa means living with enough awareness of Allah that a person tries to stay within His command and away from what displeases Him.

The opening of Surah Al-Baqarah presents guidance as especially beneficial for the muttaqin, the people of taqwa. That already tells the beginner something important: taqwa is not only the end result of guidance. It is also part of the posture by which guidance is received well.

Surah Aal-E-Imran 3:102 then calls believers to fear Allah as He should be feared and to die only in submission. Taqwa therefore belongs to the whole path, from daily restraint to the hope of a faithful ending.
