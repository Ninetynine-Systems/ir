# Foundation

## Source anchors

- Surah Aal-E-Imran 3:96-97
- Sahih al-Bukhari 1513

Hajj is one of the five pillars of Islam and an obligation upon those who are able to make the journey. Surah Aal-E-Imran 3:96-97 ties the pilgrimage to the first House established for humanity and makes clear that pilgrimage to it is due to Allah from the people for whoever is able to find a way.

This already gives the beginner the foundation: Hajj is worship, not tourism; obligation, not spectacle; and a response to a house Allah has honored since the time of Ibrahim.
