# Foundation

## Source anchors

- Surah Al-Baqarah 2:43
- Sahih al-Bukhari 24

Zakat is not optional generosity. It is an obligation bound closely to prayer in the Quran and Sunnah. Surah Al-Baqarah 2:43 joins the command to establish prayer with the command to give zakah, and the prophetic texts name it among the central obligations of Islam.

At the foundations level, zakat means a required portion taken from qualifying wealth and given where Allah has commanded. Its spirit includes purification of wealth, purification of the soul from greed, and visible care for people in need.
