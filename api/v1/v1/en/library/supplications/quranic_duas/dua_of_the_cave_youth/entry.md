# When To Use It

## Source anchors

- Surah Al-Kahf 18:10

This supplication comes from the believing youths in Surah Al-Kahf when they withdrew from pressure and placed themselves under the care of Allah. Their first request was not for worldly control. They asked for mercy and for right guidance in the matter before them.

That makes this a powerful Quranic dua for moments of moral pressure, uncertainty, relocation, social isolation, or any situation where a believer needs Allah to prepare a sound path forward. It joins trust, vulnerability, and hope in a few words.

No fixed repetition count is attached here. Recite it carefully, especially when asking Allah to open a way that is both safe and right.
