Here is a very small Dhul-Qa'dah challenge. Choose one of these before the day ends:

1. Set aside a small amount, however modest, with the intention of future sacred travel or worship.
2. Learn one foundational point about Hajj that you do not already know.
3. Make one private dua asking Allah to open a path to His House if and when it would be good for you.

> "Actions are judged by intentions..."
> - Sahih al-Bukhari, 1

You do not need to do all three. The point is not volume. The point is to let intention become something slightly more concrete today.
