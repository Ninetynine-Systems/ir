For some people, Hajj is close. For others, it still feels far away. The Quran speaks with honesty about that.

> "And [due] to Allah from the people is a pilgrimage to the House, for whoever is able to find thereto a way."
> - Aal-E-Imran, 3:97

Ability matters. Islam does not ask people to pretend they can do what they cannot. But there is still room, even before full ability, for sincere preparation.

You can begin with a little knowledge. A little saving. A little dua. A little honesty about what would need to change if Allah were to invite you.

Do not despise small beginnings. Many sacred journeys are prepared for long before they are taken.
