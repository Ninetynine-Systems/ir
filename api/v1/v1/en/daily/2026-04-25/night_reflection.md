Sometimes the path is not open yet, and all you can really offer is sincerity.

> "And whoever relies upon Allah - then He is sufficient for him."
> - At-Talaq, 65:3

That is not a guarantee that every hope appears on your timeline. It is something deeper. It means the One you are turning toward is enough even before the road becomes visible.

If a future act of worship still feels out of reach, let tonight be simple. Ask. Trust. Keep the intention clean. Allah knows the difference between neglect and longing.
