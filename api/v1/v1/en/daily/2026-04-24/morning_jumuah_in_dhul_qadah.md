Friday has arrived inside a sacred month, and that gives the morning a quiet weight.

> "O you who have believed, when [the adhan] is called for the prayer on the day of Jumu'ah, then proceed to the remembrance of Allah and leave trade."
> - Al-Jumu'ah, 62:9

Let this morning be recognizable as Friday. Slow down a little if you can. Make room for Jumu'ah before the world crowds in. Read Surah Al-Kahf today. Send blessings upon the Prophet ﷺ. Go to the prayer as someone arriving for remembrance, not merely fitting it into an already crowded schedule.

> - Special Virtues of the Qur'an's Chapters and Verses, 36
> - Sunan Abi Dawud, 1047

Dhul-Qa'dah teaches carefulness, and Jumu'ah gathers the week back to its center. It is a beautiful pairing.
