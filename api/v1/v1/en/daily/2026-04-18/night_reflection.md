I keep thinking tonight about the kind of opening Yusuf received. Not hurried. Not partial. Not an escape that left his name buried under suspicion. Allah let the truth emerge first, then made room for release.

> "For indeed, with hardship comes ease. Indeed, with hardship comes ease."
> - Al-Sharh, 94:5-6

The ease was real, but it came in the form Allah chose. That matters. We often imagine relief as speed: fix it now, open it now, remove it now. But sometimes relief arrives with restoration. Sometimes Allah does not only take you out of the hardship. He also teaches you what the hardship was preparing.

If there is something in your life that feels late, perhaps it is still being arranged with more care than you can see.

May Allah bring you ease that is clean, timely, and better than the version you would have rushed toward on your own.
