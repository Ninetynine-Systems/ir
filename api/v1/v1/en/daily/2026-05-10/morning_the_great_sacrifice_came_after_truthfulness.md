Relief in the story did not come before surrender. It came after truthfulness had been shown.

> "Indeed, this was the clear trial. And We ransomed him with a great sacrifice."
> - Surah As-Saffat, 37:106-107

Allah's openings often arrive after the servant has already turned sincerely. Not always with immediate ease, but with clarity, mercy, and preservation at the right moment.

If you are waiting for an opening, keep being truthful before Allah.
