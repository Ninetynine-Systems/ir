The road feels closer now. More pilgrims are entering the region, more families are saying goodbye, and more hearts are beginning to understand that Hajj is not far away.

> "And proclaim to the people the Hajj; they will come to you on foot and on every lean camel; they will come from every distant pass."
> - Surah Al-Hajj, 22:27

Every arrival belongs under that old call. The airports and buses are new, but the answer is ancient. Allah made Ibrahim's proclamation travel farther than any human voice could travel by itself.

If you are watching from home, do not let the season pass only as news. Let it become response. A little longing, a little dua, a little reverence. The road is drawing near, even for the heart that has not packed a bag.
