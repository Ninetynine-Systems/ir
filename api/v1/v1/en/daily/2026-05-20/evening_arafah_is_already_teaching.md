Arafah has not arrived yet in the expected schedule, but its lessons have already begun to teach.

> "And remember Him, as He has guided you, for indeed, you were before that among those astray."
> - Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:198

The verse ties remembrance to guidance. That is important. A servant does not remember Allah as someone self-made. He remembers as someone rescued from misguidance, taught, corrected, and brought near.

That should make dua humbler. The one who asks at Arafah, or from home on the day of Arafah, is not presenting a flawless record. He is coming as someone who needs guidance again and again.

Start practicing that need now.
