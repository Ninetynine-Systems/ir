The sun has risen on the last Friday of Shawwal, and by sunset tonight Dhul-Qa'dah will begin. It is one of the four sacred months, honored since the time of the Prophet ﷺ and before, and its nearness gives this day a quiet weight.

> ﴿ إِنَّ عِدَّةَ الشُّهُورِ عِندَ اللَّهِ اثْنَا عَشَرَ شَهْرًا فِي كِتَابِ اللَّهِ يَوْمَ خَلَقَ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضَ مِنْهَا أَرْبَعَةٌ حُرُمٌ ﴾
> *Inna 'iddat ash-shuhoori 'indallahi ithna 'ashara shahran fi kitabillahi yawma khalaqa as-samawati wal-arda minha arba'atun hurum*
> "Indeed, the number of months with Allah is twelve months in the book of Allah on the day He created the heavens and the earth; of these, four are sacred..."
> — At-Tawbah, 9:36

And because this morning is Jumu'ah as well as a threshold into a sacred month, another verse meets us right where we are:

> ﴿ يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا إِذَا نُودِيَ لِلصَّلَاةِ مِن يَوْمِ الْجُمُعَةِ فَاسْعَوْا إِلَىٰ ذِكْرِ اللَّهِ وَذَرُوا الْبَيْعَ ﴾
> *Ya ayyuha alladhina amanu idha nudiya lis-salati min yawmi al-jumu'ati fas'aw ila dhikri Allahi wa dharu al-bay'*
> "O you who have believed, when [the adhan] is called for the prayer on the day of Jumu'ah, then proceed to the remembrance of Allah and leave trade."
> — Al-Jumu'ah, 62:9

The Prophet ﷺ said about this day: "The best day on which the sun has risen is Friday. On it Adam was created, on it he was entered into Paradise, on it he was expelled from it, and the Hour will not be established except on Friday."

What a gentle threshold — a blessed Friday leaning toward a sacred month. If you're able, slow the morning down a little. Read Surah Al-Kahf. Send blessings upon the Prophet ﷺ. Then go to Jumu'ah with a heart that is not rushing from one thing to another, but arriving for the remembrance of Allah.

May Allah bless your Friday and bless the way you enter this sacred month tonight.
