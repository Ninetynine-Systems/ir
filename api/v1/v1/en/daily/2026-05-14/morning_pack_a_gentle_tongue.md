A person can pack everything and still forget one of the most needed provisions: a gentle tongue.

> "So whoever has made Hajj obligatory upon himself therein, there is [to be for him] no obscenity, no disobedience, and no disputing during Hajj."
> - Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:197

This ayah turns preparation inward. Hajj will place people near one another under heat, waiting, fatigue, and pressure. The test is not only whether the body reaches the rite. It is whether the character survives the road.

Even if you are staying home, take the lesson. Begin packing restraint today.
