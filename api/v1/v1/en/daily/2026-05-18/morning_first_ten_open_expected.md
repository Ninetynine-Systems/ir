If the expected calendar holds after moon sighting, these are the opening days of Dhul-Hijjah. Treat the date with humility, and treat the opportunity with seriousness.

The Prophet ﷺ said that there are no days in which righteous deeds are more beloved to Allah than these days.
> - Sahih al-Bukhari, 969

That mercy is not only for those already traveling. The pilgrim, the parent at home, the worker, the student, the tired believer trying again - all can fill these days with deeds Allah loves.

Begin simply: prayer on time, dhikr, charity, salawat, repentance, and a tongue that does less harm.
