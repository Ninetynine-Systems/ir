Night makes it easier to remember that sacred things should be approached with care.

> "And whoever honors the symbols of Allah, indeed it is from the piety of hearts."
> - Surah Al-Hajj, 22:32

You do not need to be in Makkah to begin this. Reverence can start in an ordinary room, with a lowered voice, a cleaner intention, and a quieter heart.

If you wish tonight, ask Allah for the kind of piety that treats His signs seriously before the feet ever draw near.
