As Hajj season draws nearer, many hearts begin turning toward Madinah and the wider Hijaz. Before the body reaches sacred ground, the heart often starts traveling first.

> "Our Lord, I have settled some of my descendants in an uncultivated valley near Your sacred House..."
> - Surah Ibrahim, 14:37

Ibrahim's path to the House began with trust long before arrival. Hajj is like that too. There is paperwork, packing, waiting, and long roads. But beneath all of that is a quieter beginning: a heart being turned toward Allah.

If this season is stirring something in you, let the first step be inward. Send more salawat. Leave one distraction. Make one honest dua for invitation. Some journeys begin with tickets. Others begin with longing.
