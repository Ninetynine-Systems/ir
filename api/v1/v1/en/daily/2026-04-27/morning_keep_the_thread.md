Over these ten days we moved from Yusuf's pardon into sacred-month steadiness, then into quiet preparation, dua, and Friday return. The easiest mistake now would be to let each piece remain only a past reading.

> "O you who have believed, persevere and endure and remain stationed and fear Allah that you may be successful."
> - Aal-E-Imran, 3:200

Perseverance is often less dramatic than we imagine. Sometimes it means carrying one lesson forward instead of starting over every morning. Keep one thread. Keep one dua. Keep one act of carefulness.

The heart does not need a hundred new things today. It may only need to hold onto what Allah has already opened.
