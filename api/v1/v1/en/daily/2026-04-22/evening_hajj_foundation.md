Dhul-Qa'dah naturally turns the heart toward Hajj, even before the season reaches its visible peak. The point is not to force emotion, but to remember what Hajj actually is.

> "And [due] to Allah from the people is a pilgrimage to the House, for whoever is able to find thereto a way."
> - Aal-E-Imran, 3:97

And the Prophet ﷺ counted Hajj among the five pillars of Islam.
> - Sahih al-Bukhari, 8

That means Hajj is not spiritual tourism. It is worship. It is response. It is one of the great structures Allah has placed inside the religion.

Even if you are not going this year, there is still a gentle benefit in letting your heart turn toward the House. Learn a little. Make dua for invitation. Let the existence of this pillar pull your concerns into better proportion.

Some journeys begin with footsteps. Others begin with reverence.
