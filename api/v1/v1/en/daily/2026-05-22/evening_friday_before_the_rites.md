A Friday before great days is a mercy of rehearsal.

> "That they may witness benefits for themselves and mention the name of Allah on known days..."
> - Surah Al-Hajj, 22:28

If the known days are marked by Allah's name, then let Friday teach the tongue before they arrive. Let it teach you how to leave lesser things, gather your attention, and return to remembrance without delay.

Do not treat readiness as only future. Friday was part of the readiness.
