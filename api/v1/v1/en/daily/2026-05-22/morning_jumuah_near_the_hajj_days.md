This Friday stands close to the expected Hajj days.

> "O you who have believed, when [the adhan] is called for the prayer on the day of Jumu'ah, then proceed to the remembrance of Allah and leave trade."
> - Surah Al-Jumu'ah, 62:9

Let Jumu'ah do its work on you today. Make room for Surah Al-Kahf. Increase salawat. Arrive to the prayer with a heart that is not rushing past the mercy of the hour.

When sacred days gather near, the weekly sacred day becomes even more precious.
