Another Friday has arrived while hearts remain turned toward the coming Hajj days.

> "O you who have believed, when [the adhan] is called for the prayer on the day of Jumu'ah, then proceed to the remembrance of Allah and leave trade."
> - Surah Al-Jumu'ah, 62:9

Answer Jumu'ah with presence today. Keep extra salawat near your tongue. Make room for Surah Al-Kahf. These weekly acts train the heart not to treat sacred time casually.

A believer who learns to answer the Friday call well is already being shaped for larger answers.
