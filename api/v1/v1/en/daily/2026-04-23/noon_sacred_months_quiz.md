Here is a small sacred-month check-in for the middle of the day.

1. According to At-Tawbah 9:36, how many sacred months are there in the year?
2. Which Quranic dua from Surah Al-Kahf 18:10 have we been carrying this week for mercy and right guidance?
3. What does Al-Hajj 22:32 teach us about honoring the symbols of Allah?

Answers:

1. Four.
2. `رَبَّنَا آتِنَا مِن لَّدُنكَ رَحْمَةً وَهَيِّئْ لَنَا مِنْ أَمْرِنَا رَشَدًا`
3. That honoring the symbols of Allah is from the piety of hearts.

Short quizzes like this are not about performance. They just help the heart keep what it has already been shown.
