A lot of preparation happens before anyone sees a journey begin.

You learn quietly. You save quietly. You ask forgiveness quietly. You repair intentions quietly. Even when Hajj is far from your calendar, the heart can begin to face the right direction now.

The Prophet ﷺ taught us:
> "Actions are judged by intentions..."
> - Sahih al-Bukhari, 1

That hadith is liberating. It means the path to a sacred journey does not begin only when the suitcase is packed. It begins when the intention becomes truthful.

Maybe your preparation this year is not travel. Maybe it is becoming the kind of person who would walk that journey with humility if Allah opened it for you. That too is a beginning, and beginnings matter.
