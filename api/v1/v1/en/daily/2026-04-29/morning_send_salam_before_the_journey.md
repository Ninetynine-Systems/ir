As many hearts turn toward Madinah in these days, let your tongue turn often toward salawat.

> "Indeed, Allah and His angels send blessings upon the Prophet. O you who have believed, ask [Allah to confer] blessing upon him and ask [Allah to grant him] peace."
> - Surah Al-Ahzab, 33:56

Not everyone will travel this year. Not everyone will stand in those places soon. But every believer can accompany the road with blessings upon the Messenger of Allah ﷺ.

Sometimes love is shown by motion. Sometimes it is shown by what stays on the tongue. Keep salawat close this morning.
