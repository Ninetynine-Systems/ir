Before talking about crowds and rites, Ibrahim made dua for safety and provision.

> "My Lord, make this a secure city and provide its people with fruits..."
> - Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:126

That is a beautiful order. Security. Provision. Mercy. The path to sacred worship is not separated from these needs.

If pilgrims are on your mind today, make dua for their safety. And if your own life feels unsettled, borrow Ibrahim's language. Ask Allah to place sakinah where there is unease and provision where there is fear.
