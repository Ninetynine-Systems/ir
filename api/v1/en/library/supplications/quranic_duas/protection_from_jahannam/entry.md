# When To Use It

## Source anchors

- Surah Al-Furqan 25:65-66

The Quran places this dua among the qualities of the servants of Ar-Rahman. Their worship includes hope, but it also includes fear of the Fire and dependence on Allah for protection.

Use it when the heart needs seriousness about sin, accountability, and the mercy of being saved from punishment.

No fixed repetition count is attached here. Let the words renew fear of Allah without despairing of His mercy.
