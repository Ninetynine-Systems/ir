# When To Use It

## Source anchors

- Surah Aal-E-Imran 3:8

This supplication appears on the tongues of believers who ask Allah to preserve them after guidance has already come. It teaches that being shown the truth is a mercy, but remaining firm upon it is another mercy that must also be sought.

It is fitting after learning something beneficial, after repentance, or whenever the heart fears drift, pride, confusion, or slow neglect. The dua is brief, but it gathers two essential requests together: a heart that does not turn away and a mercy that comes directly from Allah.

No fixed repetition count is attached here. Recite it with presence, humility, and a sincere need for steadiness.
