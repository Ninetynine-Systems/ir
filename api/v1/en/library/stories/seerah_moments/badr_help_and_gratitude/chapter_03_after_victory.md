# After Victory

## Source anchors

- Surah Al-Anfal 8:17-19
- Surah Al-Anfal 8:41-44

Surah Al-Anfal corrects how victory is read: it was Allah who acted, and He arranged what He willed. Even the meeting of the two sides is presented as being under divine wisdom.

A believer studies Badr to learn trust, not triumphalism. The event teaches asking Allah, obeying under pressure, recognizing weakness, and receiving success with humility. The heart that remembers Badr should leave with gratitude before it leaves with slogans.
