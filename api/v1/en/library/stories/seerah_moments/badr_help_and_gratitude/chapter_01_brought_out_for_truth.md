# Brought Out for Truth

## Source anchors

- Surah Al-Anfal 8:5-8

The Quran remembers Badr as a moment when Allah brought the believers out for truth, though some were reluctant. The passage does not flatter the believers as if courage began perfectly formed. It shows hearts being trained under Allah's command.

That honesty is part of the mercy of the Quranic account. Victory did not belong to self-confidence. It belonged to Allah, who established the truth and cut off falsehood even when the servants themselves were still being steadied.
