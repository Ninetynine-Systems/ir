# Help from Allah

## Source anchors

- Surah Al-Anfal 8:9-12
- Surah Aal Imran 3:123-127

At Badr, the believers sought help from Allah, and Allah answered. Surah Aal Imran reminds them that Allah helped them at Badr while they were weak, then directs them to have taqwa and be grateful. The mention of angelic support is tied to reassurance and Allah's command, not to spectacle.

The lesson is direct: means do not replace dua, and unseen help does not make people independent. Allah grants support so hearts become firm and so gratitude grows after fear.
