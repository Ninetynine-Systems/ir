# Mercy and Consultation

## Source anchors

- Surah Aal Imran 3:152-159

Surah Aal Imran names disobedience and dispute after Allah had shown the believers what they loved. Yet the passage does not end by crushing them. Allah tells His Messenger that by mercy from Allah he was gentle with them, and commands pardon, seeking forgiveness for them, and consultation.

This is one of Uhud's deepest lessons. Accountability and mercy are not opposites. A community can admit failure, seek forgiveness, repair obedience, and continue under prophetic gentleness. The wound becomes a path back to Allah when it produces humility.
