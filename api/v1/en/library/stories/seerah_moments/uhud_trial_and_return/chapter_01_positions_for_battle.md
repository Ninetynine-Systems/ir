# Positions for Battle

## Source anchors

- Surah Aal Imran 3:121-129

The Quran opens the Uhud passage by recalling when the Prophet left his family early to position the believers for battle. It then turns attention to hearts: groups nearly lost courage, yet Allah was their protector. The account is not interested in spectacle; it is interested in reliance.

Uhud teaches that a community may be sincere and still be tested. The first lesson is not to imagine believers as untouched by fear, but to see where fear must be carried: back to Allah, the Protector.
