# After the Wound

## Source anchors

- Surah Aal Imran 3:139-148

After loss and injury, the Quran told the believers not to weaken or grieve, and reminded them that days are turned among people. The trial exposed, purified, and taught. It did not mean Allah had abandoned them.

The verses also remember earlier believers who fought alongside prophets and did not collapse because of what struck them. Their response was dua, seeking forgiveness, firmness, and help. Uhud therefore becomes a school of recovery: pain should drive the servant toward Allah, not away from Him.
