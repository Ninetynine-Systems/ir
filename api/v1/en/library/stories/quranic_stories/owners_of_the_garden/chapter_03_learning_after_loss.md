# Learning After Loss

## Source anchors

- Surah Al-Qalam 68:30-33

After blaming themselves, the owners hoped that their Lord would replace what they lost with something better. The Quran then says that such is the punishment, while the punishment of the Hereafter is greater if only people knew.

The story is not only about a garden. It is about gratitude, social responsibility, and remembering Allah before profit narrows the soul. A believer learns to say insha'Allah, to give the needy their due, and to treat wealth as an entrusted test rather than a private fortress.
