# A Plan Without Exception

## Source anchors

- Surah Al-Qalam 68:17-24

Surah Al-Qalam tells of people who owned a garden and resolved to harvest it early while preventing the needy from receiving any share. Their plan was quiet, calculated, and confident. What was missing was remembrance of Allah and mercy toward those who had a right to compassion.

The Quran does not give names because the lesson does not need them. Wealth can make a person feel efficient while the heart becomes narrow. A plan may look organized and still be spiritually broken.
