# The Garden Changed

## Source anchors

- Surah Al-Qalam 68:19-29

While they slept, a visitation from their Lord passed over the garden. In the morning they went out quietly, thinking they were in control, only to find the garden changed. At first they thought they had lost their way; then the truth became clear.

Their words shifted from confidence to recognition. One among them reminded the others that they should glorify Allah. They admitted their wrongdoing. The story shows how quickly possession can become loss, and how mercy remains in being awakened before the final account.
