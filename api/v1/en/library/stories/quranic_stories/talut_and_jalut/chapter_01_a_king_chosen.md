# A King Chosen

## Source anchors

- Surah Al-Baqarah 2:246-248

The Quran begins this account with a community asking their prophet to appoint a king so they could fight in Allah's cause. When Talut was appointed, some objected because they measured worth by wealth and status. Their prophet reminded them that Allah had chosen him and increased him in knowledge and strength.

The story immediately corrects a common mistake. Leadership before Allah is not built only on public preference, family expectation, or visible wealth. Allah gives authority to whom He wills, and He knows the qualities people may overlook.
