# Victory by Permission

## Source anchors

- Surah Al-Baqarah 2:250-251

When they faced Jalut and his forces, the believers asked Allah to pour patience upon them, make their feet firm, and grant victory. Then Allah granted victory, and Dawud killed Jalut. The Quran keeps the outcome tied to Allah's permission, not human self-display.

This ending teaches courage without arrogance. Means matter, leadership matters, and firmness matters, but victory belongs to Allah. The servant prepares, obeys, asks, and then reads the outcome through reliance instead of pride.
