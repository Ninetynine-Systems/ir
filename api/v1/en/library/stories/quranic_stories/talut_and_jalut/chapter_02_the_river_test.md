# The River Test

## Source anchors

- Surah Al-Baqarah 2:249

Talut set out with the soldiers and announced a test at the river. The instruction was specific, and most failed it. A smaller group crossed with him, and even among them some felt the weight of the enemy before them.

The Quran does not turn the scene into spectacle. It shows that obedience can be tested through restraint, and that victory is not promised to the loudest crowd. Those who were certain they would meet Allah answered with a sentence that steadies every generation: many a small company has overcome a large company by Allah's permission.
