# Foundation

## Source anchors

- Surah Al-Baqarah 2:183-185

Fasting in Ramadan is one of the great acts of worship Allah prescribed for this ummah. Surah Al-Baqarah 2:183 states its purpose with clarity: fasting was prescribed so that believers may attain taqwa. The practice is not hunger for its own sake. It is a school of restraint, remembrance, and obedience.

The same passage ties Ramadan to revelation. It is the month in which the Quran was sent down as guidance for humanity and clear proofs of guidance and criterion. So sawm is linked to both discipline and divine speech.
