# Key Texts

## Source anchors

- Surah At-Tawbah 9:60
- Sahih al-Bukhari 1403

Surah At-Tawbah 9:60 names the recipient categories of zakat. That keeps the obligation from becoming a vague personal preference. Zakat is an act of worship with a divinely taught channel, not a donation we redirect however we like.

The Sunnah also warns against withholding it. Sahih al-Bukhari 1403 includes stern warning for the owner of wealth who does not pay its zakat. The point is not to turn the subject into fear alone, but to remind the believer that zakat belongs to Allah before it belongs to our plans.
