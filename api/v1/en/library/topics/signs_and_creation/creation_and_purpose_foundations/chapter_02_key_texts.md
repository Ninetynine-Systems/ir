# Key Texts

## Source anchors

- Surah Al-Anbiya 21:30
- Surah Al-Mu'minun 23:12-16

Surah Al-Anbiya 21:30 points to the heavens, earth, and living things as signs for faith. Surah Al-Mu'minun 23:12-16 speaks of the stages of human creation and then moves toward death and resurrection. The lesson is not pride in biology, but humility before the One who creates.

A foundations-level topic does not use these verses to make technical claims beyond the text. It uses them as revelation uses them: to awaken the heart, place human life inside Allah's power, and connect beginnings to return.
