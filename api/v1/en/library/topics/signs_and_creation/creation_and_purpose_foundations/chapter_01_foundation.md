# Foundation

## Source anchors

- Surah Adh-Dhariyat 51:56
- Surah Al-Mulk 67:2

The Quran does not leave human life floating without purpose. Surah Adh-Dhariyat 51:56 states the purpose of jinn and humankind as worship of Allah. Surah Al-Mulk 67:2 frames life and death as a test, so that deeds reveal who is best in conduct.

That means creation is not only something to admire. It is a setting for servitude. The believer looks at existence and asks a practical question: how should a created servant live before the Creator who gave life, measures time, and will bring the servant back for account?
