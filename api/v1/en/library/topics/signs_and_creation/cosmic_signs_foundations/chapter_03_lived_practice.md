# Lived Practice

## Source anchors

- Surah Yunus 10:5
- Surah Al-Mulk 67:3-5

A Muslim does not need extraordinary claims to benefit from the heavens. Fajr enters with the morning, the months are known by the moon, and repeated days keep inviting the servant back to prayer, gratitude, and accountability.

Let the sky make the heart smaller before Allah, not louder with unsupported claims. When you see sunrise, sunset, the moon's phases, or the night sky, turn the observation into worship: praise the Creator, keep time honestly, and remember that the order above you is a mercy you did not design.
