# Key Texts

## Source anchors

- Surah Al-Anbiya 21:47
- Surah Al-Inshiqaq 84:7-12
- Surah Al-Qari'ah 101:6-11

The Quran speaks of just scales, written records, and outcomes that no person can dismiss. Surah Al-Anbiya 21:47 promises that the scales of justice will be set. Surah Al-Inshiqaq contrasts the one given the record in the right hand with the one receiving it behind the back. Surah Al-Qari'ah turns the balance of deeds into a direct warning.

These passages are not abstract. They train the conscience before the Day arrives.
