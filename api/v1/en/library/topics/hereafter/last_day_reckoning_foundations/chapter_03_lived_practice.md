# Lived Practice

## Source anchors

- Surah Az-Zalzalah 99:7-8
- Surah Al-Anbiya 21:47

Belief in reckoning makes small deeds meaningful. A hidden kindness, a guarded word, a private repentance, or a restrained sin is not lost with Allah. At the same time, a small wrong is not dismissed because people ignore it.

The practical path is balance: hope in Allah's mercy, fear accountability, repair wrongs, and keep returning. The Last Day is meant to wake the heart before it is too late, not to make it despair while the door of repentance remains open.
