# Foundation

## Source anchors

- Surah Az-Zumar 39:68
- Surah Az-Zalzalah 99:1-8

The Last Day is not a mood of fear added to Islam. It is a revealed certainty that gives life moral weight. Surah Az-Zumar 39:68 speaks of the trumpet and resurrection by Allah's command. Surah Az-Zalzalah 99:1-8 brings the reminder close: even an atom's weight of good or evil will be seen.

This foundation keeps the topic sober. We do not map every headline to prophecy or chase speculative timelines. We believe what Allah revealed, prepare with repentance and obedience, and leave the unseen timing to Him.
