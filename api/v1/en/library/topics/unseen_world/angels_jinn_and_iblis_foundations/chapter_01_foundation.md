# Foundation

## Source anchors

- Surah At-Tahrim 66:6
- Surah Al-Kahf 18:50

The unseen is learned through revelation, not guesswork. Angels are servants of Allah who do not disobey what He commands them. Jinn are another created kind, morally accountable, and Iblis is identified in Surah Al-Kahf 18:50 as being from the jinn.

A beginner-safe foundation keeps categories clear. Angels are not independent powers. Jinn are not a reason for superstition. Iblis is not a rival to Allah. All are created, limited, and under Allah's authority.
