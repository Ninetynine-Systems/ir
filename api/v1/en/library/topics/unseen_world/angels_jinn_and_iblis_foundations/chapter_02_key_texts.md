# Key Texts

## Source anchors

- Surah Al-Baqarah 2:30-39
- Surah Al-Hijr 15:26-44

The account of Adam teaches more than origin. Allah honored Adam, taught him, commanded the angels to prostrate, and exposed the arrogance of Iblis when he refused. The Quran does not need folklore to make the lesson powerful. Pride before Allah is ruin, and repentance after sin is a door of mercy.

Surah Al-Hijr repeats the creation and refusal with a warning: Iblis invites, but he does not overpower Allah's sincere servants. The danger is real, yet it remains limited by Allah's decree.
