# Foundation

## Source anchors

- Surah An-Nisa 4:136
- Sahih al-Bukhari 50
- Sahih Muslim 8

Iman is not a vague religious mood. In Hadith Jibril, the Prophet defined it through its foundations: belief in Allah, His angels, His books, His messengers, the Last Day, and divine decree. The Quran echoes that call in Surah An-Nisa 4:136 by commanding the believers to believe in Allah, His Messenger, and the scriptures He sent down.

So faith begins with what Allah has revealed about Himself and the unseen. It is received, not invented. A beginner-safe understanding of iman starts here: to know what Allah and His Messenger taught us to believe, and to hold to it with submission.
