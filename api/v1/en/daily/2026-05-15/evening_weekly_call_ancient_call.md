There is a mercy in repeated calls. They keep the heart from becoming unresponsive.

> "And proclaim to the people the Hajj..."
> - Surah Al-Hajj, 22:27

A servant does not become ready for great sacred moments by ignoring smaller ones. Jumu'ah, daily prayers, a morning dhikr, a private dua - these are not separate from Hajj's spirit. They are training in response.

If Friday softened you today, let that softness travel with you into the coming week. The heart that answers often learns to answer well.
