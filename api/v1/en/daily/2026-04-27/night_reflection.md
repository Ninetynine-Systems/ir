As this ten-day stretch closes, I keep returning to a simple promise:

> "So remember Me; I will remember you..."
> - Al-Baqarah, 2:152

That is enough to walk forward with. Not that you will remember everything perfectly. Not that every feeling will stay bright. Only that remembrance is never one-sided. When a servant turns toward Allah, Allah does not leave that turning unanswered.

Take one thing from these days into tomorrow. One verse. One dua. One softer way of speaking. One honest intention. That is how continuity is built: not by trying to carry everything, but by carrying something faithfully.
