There is something humbling about the phrase "every distant pass."

> "They will come from every distant pass."
> - Surah Al-Hajj, 22:27

It reminds us that Allah's call does not belong to one people, one language, one class, or one road. The House gathers the distant. It gathers the tired. It gathers those who cross water, desert, border, age, fear, and weakness to answer.

That should make the believer less self-centered. Hajj widens the heart. It reminds us that our private worship is part of a much larger ummah answering one Lord.

Tonight, make dua for the pilgrims whose names you do not know. They are your people too.
