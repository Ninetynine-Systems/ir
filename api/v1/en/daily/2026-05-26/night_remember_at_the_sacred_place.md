As night falls over the expected Arafah day, return to remembrance.

> "Remember Allah at al-Mash'ar al-Haram."
> - Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:198

A day of dua should not end in emptiness. Let it end with Allah's name, with forgiveness on the tongue, and with hope that He accepted more than you could see.

Remember Him as He guided you.
