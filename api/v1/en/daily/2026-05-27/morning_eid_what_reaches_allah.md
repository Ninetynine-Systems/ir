If the expected calendar holds, today is Eid al-Adha. Allah knows the confirmed sighting, and He knows the hearts behind every sacrifice.

> "Their meat will not reach Allah, nor will their blood, but what reaches Him is piety from you."
> - Surah Al-Hajj, 22:37

Eid is not a day for empty display. It is a day to remember that Allah looks beyond the visible form to the taqwa beneath it.

Let the joy be real, but let it stay humble. Let gratitude fill the house. Let generosity reach others. And let the heart ask: what part of me is reaching Allah today?
