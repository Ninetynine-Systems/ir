Purpose does not always arrive as a big feeling. Sometimes it arrives as Fajr, honesty, restraint, or service.

> Source: Surah Adh-Dhariyat, 51:56; Sahih al-Bukhari 1

Do not rush past the anchor. Read it as a small direction for the next few hours, not as a slogan. The day does not need to become dramatic; it only needs one place where remembrance changes your response.

Let this reminder enter the first part of the day through one small, sincere action.
