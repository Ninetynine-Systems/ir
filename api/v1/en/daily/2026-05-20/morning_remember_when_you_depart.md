Even the movement after Arafah is tied to remembrance.

> "But when you depart from Arafat, remember Allah at al-Mash'ar al-Haram. And remember Him, as He has guided you..."
> - Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:198

The ayah does not let the servant pass from one place to another empty-hearted. Movement itself becomes a container for dhikr.

That is a lesson before the day arrives. When you move from task to task today, do not let your heart become vacant. Remember Him as He has guided you.
