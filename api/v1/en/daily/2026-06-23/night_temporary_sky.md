Choose preparation over fear: repent, forgive, and put one right thing back in its place before sleep.

> Source: Surah At-Takwir, 81:1-2; Surah Al-Qiyamah, 75:7-9; Surah Al-Infitar, 82:1-2

Let the source set the scale of the night. Do not turn regret into despair or reflection into self-punishment. Return to Allah with what is true, and leave what you cannot fix in His care.

Close the day gently: ask, return, and rest with your heart facing Allah.
