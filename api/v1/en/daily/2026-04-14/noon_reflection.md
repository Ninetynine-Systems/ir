A quiet question for your afternoon.

> ﴿ وَإِذْ تَأَذَّنَ رَبُّكُمْ لَئِن شَكَرْتُمْ لَأَزِيدَنَّكُمْ ﴾
> *Wa idh ta'adhanna Rabbukum la-in shakartum la-azidannakum*
> "And when your Lord proclaimed: 'If you are grateful, I will surely increase you.'"
> — Ibrahim, 14:7

What's one small thing you could thank Allah for right now? Not the big milestones. The tiny, easily missed ones.

A working body. A breath. A moment of peace.

Say Alhamdulillah. Let it land.
