Some preparations are meant to make the trip smoother. Taqwa is meant to make the servant better.

> "And take provisions, but indeed, the best provision is taqwa."
> - Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:197

That is why sacred travel is not measured only by how organized a person is. It is measured by what the journey is doing to the heart. Is it making you more patient? Less sharp-tongued? More reliant on Allah?

Even from home, you can prepare in that way.
