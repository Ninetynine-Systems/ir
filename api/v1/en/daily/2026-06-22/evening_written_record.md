Self-accounting is mercy when it comes early. Review the day before it hardens: thank Allah, seek forgiveness, and repair what can still be repaired.

> Source: Surah Al-Haqqah, 69:19-37; Surah Az-Zalzalah, 99:7-8

Keep the claim modest and close to the source. No extra story has to be added for the reminder to work. Let the verse or report narrow the ego and widen mercy in the next conversation.

Let the evening turn the source into practice, not just an idea to admire from a distance.
