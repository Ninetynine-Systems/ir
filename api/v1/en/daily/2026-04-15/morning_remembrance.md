Wednesday morning.

Two days left in Shawwal. If you're among those still hoping to complete the six days, the window is narrowing but it's not closed.

The Prophet ﷺ didn't specify that they must be consecutive. Any six days of Shawwal fulfill the sunnah. So if you have two days left in you — today and tomorrow, or today and Friday — that still counts for something beautiful.

> ﴿ إِنَّ مَعَ الْعُسْرِ يُسْرًا ﴾
> *Inna ma'al-'usri yusra*
> "Indeed, with hardship comes ease."
> — Ash-Sharh, 94:6

Ease is built into the system. Even in the striving, there's relief.

If you can fast today, may Allah make it light. If you cannot, may Allah reward your intention multiplied. He is Al-Karim — the Most Generous — and His generosity has no cap.
