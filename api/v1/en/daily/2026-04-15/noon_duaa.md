Quick midday breath.

Try this: Close your eyes for just ten seconds. Breathe in slowly. Breathe out slower.

Now say:

> ﴿ لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ سُبْحَانَكَ إِنِّي كُنتُ مِنَ الظَّالِمِينَ ﴾
> *La ilaha illa Anta subhanaka inni kuntu minaz-zalimin*
> "There is no deity except You; exalted are You. Indeed, I have been of the wrongdoers."
> — Al-Anbiya, 21:87

The Prophet ﷺ said this is the duaa of Yunus عليه السلام when he was in the belly of the whale. He said no Muslim makes this duaa for anything except that Allah answers it.
