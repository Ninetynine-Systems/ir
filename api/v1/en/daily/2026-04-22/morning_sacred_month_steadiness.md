Now that the Yusuf arc has come to rest, the path ahead feels quieter. Dhul-Qa'dah is often like that. It does not arrive with the intensity of Ramadan or the public motion of Hajj. It teaches steadiness.

> "Unquestionably, by the remembrance of Allah hearts find rest."
> - Ar-Ra'd, 13:28

Rest is not always found by changing your circumstances. Sometimes it is found by returning your heart to its true center again and again, especially in a month that invites reverence without spectacle.

If the last few days stirred something in you, do not rush to replace it with noise. Let it settle. Let one small remembrance stay with you through the day. Let calm itself become worship.
