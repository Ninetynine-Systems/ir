As we move from Yusuf's story into a quieter stretch of sacred time, this is a fitting dua to keep close.

> ﴿ رَبَّنَا آتِنَا مِن لَّدُنكَ رَحْمَةً وَهَيِّئْ لَنَا مِنْ أَمْرِنَا رَشَدًا ﴾
> Rabbana atina min ladunka rahmatan wa hayyi' lana min amrina rashada.
> "Our Lord, grant us from Yourself mercy and prepare for us from our affair right guidance."
> - Al-Kahf, 18:10

The believing youths asked not only for mercy, but for the right shaping of what lay ahead. That is such a balanced prayer: tenderness from Allah, and a sound path through what is coming.

If you are entering a new phase and do not yet know its full shape, keep this dua near your tongue today.
