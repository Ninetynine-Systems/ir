It is striking that the builders of the House still asked Allah to teach them.

> "And show us our rites and accept our repentance."
> - Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:128

This is the opposite of religious arrogance. They were in the middle of obedience, yet they did not assume self-sufficiency. The more sacred the task, the more they felt their need.

That should soften us. Many people now are taking classes, reading guides, asking questions, and preparing carefully before travel. That is not a sign of weakness. It is part of prophetic humility.

The true traveler is not the one who feels ready by himself. It is the one who knows he must be shown.
