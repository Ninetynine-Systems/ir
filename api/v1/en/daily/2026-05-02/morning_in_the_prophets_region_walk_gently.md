These days naturally make many hearts think of Madinah.

> "Indeed, Allah and His angels send blessings upon the Prophet..."
> - Surah Al-Ahzab, 33:56

When the city of the Prophet ﷺ comes to mind, let that thought make you gentler. Let it increase salawat. Let it refine your speech.

The road toward sacred places should not make us louder inside. It should make us more careful. If your heart has turned toward Madinah today, accompany that turn with blessings and adab.
