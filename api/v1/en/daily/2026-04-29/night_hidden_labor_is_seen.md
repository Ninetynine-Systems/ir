A day of preparation can feel ordinary from the outside.

> "Our Lord, accept [this] from us. Indeed, You are the Hearing, the Knowing."
> - Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:127

Yet Allah hears the quiet labor that people rarely praise: the planning, the learning, the repentance, the private effort to make intention cleaner than appearance.

Sleep tonight with the dua of Ibrahim and Ismail on your tongue. Hidden work is not hidden from Allah.
