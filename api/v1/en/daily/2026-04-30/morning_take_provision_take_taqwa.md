Preparation has visible and invisible forms.

> "And take provisions, but indeed, the best provision is taqwa."
> - Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:197

People preparing for Hajj think about passports, medicines, bags, schedules, and strength. All of that matters. But Allah directs the heart to a deeper supply: taqwa.

Ask yourself this morning what provision you are gathering that no airport can weigh and no hand can pack. Patience. Sincerity. Restraint. Reliance. These are not secondary items. They are the part that lasts.
