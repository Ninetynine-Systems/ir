Jumu'ah afternoon.

If you haven't prayed yet, this is your gentle nudge. The mid-afternoon window is still open.

A sunnah to consider: arriving early. The Prophet ﷺ said that whoever comes early on Friday, it is as if he sacrificed a camel. Then a cow. Then a sheep...

Even a little early counts.

> ﴿ خَيْرُ يَوْمٍ طَلَعَتْ عَلَيْهِ الشَّمْسُ يَوْمُ الْجُمُعَةِ ﴾
> *Khayru yawmin tala'at 'alayhi-sh-shamsu yawmul-jumu'ah*
> "The best day on which the sun rises is Friday."
> — Sahih Muslim, 854

The best day. Treat it like one, even in small ways.

See you on the other side of the prayer.
