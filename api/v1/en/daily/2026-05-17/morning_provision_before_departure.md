Before the expected Dhul-Hijjah window opens, return once more to the ayah of provision.

> "And take provisions, but indeed, the best provision is taqwa."
> - Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:197

It is possible to prepare outwardly and remain inwardly thin. It is also possible for someone far from the road to gather a provision Allah loves.

Today, collect what cannot be photographed: a guarded tongue, a sincere apology, a little more patience, a hidden good deed.
