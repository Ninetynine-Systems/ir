Yusuf was prepared in a prison. Yunus was heard in darkness. Not every place of confinement is empty with Allah.

> "So We responded to him and saved him from the distress. And thus do We save the believers."
> - Al-Anbiya, 21:88

That promise does not erase pain, and it does not mean every rescue looks immediate. But it does teach us something essential: the hidden place is still under Allah's care.

Over the last days we watched Yusuf's story close with gratitude. Today Yunus reminds us that even before the closing, while the distress is still all around, a servant can return to Allah with words that are completely true.

If your life has a room in it that still feels narrow, do not assume nothing holy can happen there.
