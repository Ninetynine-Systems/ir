There is a subtle danger in sacred travel: the route can become more exciting than the One the route is meant to teach you to love.

> "O you who have believed, ask [Allah to confer] blessing upon him and ask [Allah to grant him] peace."
> - Surah Al-Ahzab, 33:56

If thoughts of Madinah are filling hearts right now, let them deepen love for the Messenger of Allah ﷺ, not just fascination with place. The true fruit is not merely having seen a city. The true fruit is that salawat increases, manners soften, and obedience becomes more beloved.

Love that does not reform the servant is still incomplete.
