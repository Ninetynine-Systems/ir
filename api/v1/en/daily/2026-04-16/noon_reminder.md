This afternoon, a reminder.

Dhul-Qa'dah begins tomorrow. One of the four sacred months — a time when good deeds are heavier and harmful actions more serious.

> ﴿ إِنَّ عِدَّةَ الشُّهُورِ عِندَ اللَّهِ اثْنَا عَشَرَ شَهْرًا فِي كِتَابِ اللَّهِ يَوْمَ خَلَقَ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضَ مِنْهَا أَرْبَعَةٌ حُرُمٌ ﴾
> *Inna 'iddata-sh-shuhuri 'indallahi ithna 'ashara shahran fi kitabillahi yawma khalaqas-samawati wal-arda minha arba'atun hurum*
> "Indeed, the number of months with Allah is twelve months in the register of Allah from the day He created the heavens and the earth; of these, four are sacred."
> — At-Tawbah, 9:36

The sacred months are Dhul-Qa'dah, Dhul-Hijjah, Muharram, and Rajab. Times to be extra mindful, extra gentle, extra generous.

Something to sit with as we transition.
