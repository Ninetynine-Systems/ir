Thursday morning.

Last day of Shawwal. Tomorrow a new month begins — Dhul-Qa'dah, one of the four sacred months.

If you have one fast left in you for today, it completes something beautiful. If you've already completed the six, or if circumstances didn't allow it this year — your effort, your intention, your Ramadan itself is treasured.

> ﴿ فَمَن تَطَوَّعَ خَيْرًا فَهُوَ خَيْرٌ لَّهُ ﴾
> *Fa-man tatwwa'a khayran fahuwa khayrun lahu*
> "And whoever volunteers good — it is better for him."
> — Al-Baqarah, 2:184

Voluntary good deeds are never wasted. They rise. They accumulate. They become the weight on your scale you never expected.

Whatever today holds for you, may it be blessed. And may we enter the sacred month tomorrow with hearts that are a little softer than they were.

How are you feeling as Shawwal closes?
