A quick pause for your afternoon.

The Prophet ﷺ taught us to say between Dhuhr and Asr:

> ﴿ سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ وَبِحَمْدِهِ ﴾
> *Subhanallahi wa bihamdihi*
> "Glory is to Allah and praise is to Him."

Even once. Even quietly under your breath while you're walking or waiting.

It's not about quantity. It's about returning.
