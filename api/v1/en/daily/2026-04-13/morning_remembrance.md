Monday morning.

I was sitting with my tea earlier, watching the light change, and I kept thinking about beginnings. The Prophet ﷺ used to say that Allah loves consistent deeds, even if they're small. Not the grand gestures that burn out in a week. The quiet ones that become part of who you are.

> ﴿ إِنَّ اللَّهَ يُحِبُّ الْمُحْسِنِينَ ﴾
> *Innallaha yuhibbul-muhsineen*
> "Indeed, Allah loves those who do good."
> — Al-Baqarah, 2:195

This week, what if you chose one small thing and just... kept it? Not to prove anything. Just because it feels right.

What comes to mind?
