I woke up this morning thinking about beginnings. How every journey starts with a single step, a single breath, a single moment of intention. The dawn light was just beginning to filter through when I remembered the words of our Messenger ﷺ about consistent deeds.

> ﴿ إِنَّ اللَّهَ يُحِبُّ الْمُحْسِنِينَ ﴾
> *Innallaha yuhibbul-muhsineen*
> "Indeed, Allah loves those who do good."
> — Al-Baqarah, 2:195

The Prophet ﷺ taught us that Allah loves consistent deeds, even small ones. Not the grand gestures that burn bright and fade fast, but the quiet practices that become part of who we are. A single ayah after Fajr. A moment of gratitude before sleep. These small anchors hold us steady.

What small thing might you choose today — not to prove anything, but simply because it brings you closer to Allah?
