If the expected schedule holds, many pilgrims will begin the first Hajj movements tomorrow. Tonight belongs to humility.

> "Our Lord, accept [this] from us. Indeed, You are the Hearing, the Knowing."
> - Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:127

The night before a sacred act is not only for reviewing plans. It is for lowering the heart before Allah. The servant has means, but Allah grants acceptance. The servant prepares, but Allah opens the path.

Whether you are near the rites or far from them, make this dua your shelter tonight.
