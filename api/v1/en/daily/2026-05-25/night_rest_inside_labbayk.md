A day of answer can end in quiet surrender.

> "Here I am, O Allah, here I am..."
> - Sahih al-Bukhari, 1549; Sahih Muslim, 1184

Let "Labbayk" become more than a sound. Let it become rest: I am where You called me to be, doing what You gave me to do, needing what only You can give.

Sleep under that answer.
