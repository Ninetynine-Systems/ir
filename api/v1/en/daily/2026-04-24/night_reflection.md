The week has moved from Yusuf's forgiveness to sacred-month reminders, from duas to preparation, and now to Friday. There is a thread running through all of it: return.

> "Unquestionably, by the remembrance of Allah hearts find rest."
> - Ar-Ra'd, 13:28

Rest does not always mean the world quiets down. Sometimes it means the heart remembers where to go when the world does not quiet down.

Carry one thing from this Friday into the next week. One line. One dua. One softened intention. That is enough for tonight.
