Relief came to Yusuf عليه السلام, but it did not arrive as ease alone. It arrived as trust, responsibility, and service.

> "He said, 'Appoint me over the storehouses of the land. Indeed, I am a knowing guardian.'"
> - সূরা ইউসুফ, 12:55

That is worth pausing over tonight. Yusuf did not emerge from prison merely to breathe easier. He emerged ready to carry something for others.

Sometimes we imagine that if Allah removes our hardship, the next stage will simply be rest. But often the next stage is usefulness. The waiting softens you, teaches you, empties you of illusion, and then Allah places something in your hands that you could not have carried before.

If Allah has brought you through something difficult, perhaps the question is not only "What have I been spared?" but also "What have I been prepared for?"
