The Quran does not let Arafah end without forgiveness.

> "Then depart from the place from where [all] the people depart and ask forgiveness of Allah. Indeed, Allah is Forgiving and Merciful."
> - Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:199

After standing, after asking, after the immense gathering, the servant is told to seek forgiveness. That is so fitting. The more Allah allows a person to worship, the more that person sees his need for pardon.

Do not leave a sacred day impressed with yourself. Leave asking forgiveness. Leave with gratitude. Leave knowing that if anything was accepted, it was mercy.
