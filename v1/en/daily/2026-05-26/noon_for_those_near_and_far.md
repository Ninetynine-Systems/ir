At noon, remember the wideness of Allah's mercy.

The Prophet ﷺ said there is no day on which Allah frees more servants from the Fire than the Day of Arafah.
> - Sahih Muslim, 1348

Do not shrink the day into your own emotional state. If you feel soft, ask. If you feel dry, ask. If you feel far, ask.

The door of mercy is larger than the condition you woke up in.
