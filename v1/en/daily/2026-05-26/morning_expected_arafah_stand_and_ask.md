If the expected calendar holds, today is the Day of Arafah. Allah knows the confirmed moon-sighting, and He knows every servant standing, weeping, waiting, and asking.

> "But when you depart from Arafat, remember Allah at al-Mash'ar al-Haram. And remember Him, as He has guided you..."
> - Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:198

Arafah is a day to remember that guidance itself is mercy. No one stands before Allah with a self-made heart. We come because He called, taught, guided, and allowed another chance.

Whether you are near the plain or far away, make the morning honest. Ask as someone who needs Allah completely.
