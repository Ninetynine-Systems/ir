Arrival is a mercy, but it is not the final measure.

> "Their meat will not reach Allah, nor will their blood, but what reaches Him is piety from you."
> - Surah Al-Hajj, 22:37

The Quran keeps teaching us not to stop at the visible form. Sacred movement, sacrifice, clothing, crowds, and emotion all need taqwa to become worship that reaches Allah.

That should humble the traveler and console the one who stayed home. Allah is not reached by distance crossed alone. What reaches Him is piety.

So keep preparing the part of you that no one can photograph.
