The expected arrival phase is closing around these days, but spiritual arrival is still being prepared.

> "And whoever honors the symbols of Allah, indeed it is from the piety of hearts."
> - Surah Al-Hajj, 22:32

A person may arrive early and still need the heart to catch up. Another may remain far away and still honor Allah's symbols with trembling sincerity.

Do not measure only by location. Measure by reverence, humility, remembrance, and care.
