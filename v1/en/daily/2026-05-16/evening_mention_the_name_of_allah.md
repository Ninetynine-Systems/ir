A season can become crowded with religious information and still become thin in remembrance.

> "...and mention the name of Allah on known days..."
> - Surah Al-Hajj, 22:28

That is worth noticing. Learning matters. Logistics matter. But the center is dhikr. A person can know many details and still forget the One for whom the journey exists.

Keep returning to the simple center: Allahu akbar. Alhamdulillah. La ilaha illa Allah. Subhan Allah. Salawat upon the Prophet ﷺ.

Sacred knowledge should lead the tongue back to sacred remembrance.
