The known days are approaching, and the heart should not arrive at them distracted.

> "That they may witness benefits for themselves and mention the name of Allah on known days..."
> - Surah Al-Hajj, 22:28

The verse ties the benefits of Hajj to remembrance. Not only movement. Not only gathering. Not only awe. The Name of Allah must remain central.

Begin guarding that now. Let the coming days find you already practicing remembrance, not trying to recover it at the last moment.
