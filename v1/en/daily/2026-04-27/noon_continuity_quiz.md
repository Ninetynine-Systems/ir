Let's close this stretch with a small continuity quiz.

1. What words of pardon did Yusuf say to his brothers in Surah Yusuf 12:92?
2. Which dua from Aal-E-Imran 3:8 asks Allah not to let our hearts deviate after guidance?
3. What does Al-Hajj 22:32 say about honoring the symbols of Allah?
4. What should a Friday morning reminder include according to the rhythm we have been keeping this week?

Answers:

1. `لَا تَثْرِيبَ عَلَيْكُمُ الْيَوْمَ`
2. `رَبَّنَا لَا تُزِغْ قُلُوبَنَا بَعْدَ إِذْ هَدَيْتَنَا...`
3. That honoring the symbols of Allah is from the piety of hearts.
4. A clear turn toward Jumu'ah, with space for Surah Al-Kahf and remembrance before the prayer.

Memory grows through revisiting, not only through reading once.
