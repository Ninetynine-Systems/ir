As the Ibrahim arc deepens, the heart reaches one of its heaviest lessons.

> "[Abraham] said, 'O my son, indeed I have seen in a dream that I must sacrifice you, so see what you think.'"
> - Surah As-Saffat, 37:102

Some acts of obedience become clear only after surrender, not before. Ibrahim did not begin with complete ease. He began with truthfulness before Allah.

There are places in every life where the servant is asked to put submission ahead of full emotional comfort. That is never light. But it is part of the road of nearness.
