There is a tenderness in remembering that distress does not cancel nearness.

> "Say, 'O My servants who have transgressed against themselves, do not despair of the mercy of Allah...'"
> - Az-Zumar, 39:53

Tonight does not have to hold a complete answer. It may be enough to refuse despair, to keep the dua on your tongue, and to leave the unopened matter with Allah one more time.

Mercy does not always arrive noisily. Sometimes it begins as the ability to keep turning back.
