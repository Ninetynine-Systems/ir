Eid joy should carry remembrance, not forgetfulness.

> "So remember Me; I will remember you; and be grateful to Me and do not deny Me."
> - Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:152

After mercy, gratitude. After dua, remembrance. After Arafah, a heart that does not rush back to heedlessness.

Let the day be generous and glad, but keep Allah's name near.
