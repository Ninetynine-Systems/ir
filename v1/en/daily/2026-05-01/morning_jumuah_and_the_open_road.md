This Friday arrives while the Hajj season is beginning to stir.

> "O you who have believed, when [the adhan] is called for the prayer on the day of Jumu'ah, then proceed to the remembrance of Allah and leave trade."
> - Surah Al-Jumu'ah, 62:9

Jumu'ah trains the heart to answer sacred appointments without delay. That is one of the quiet links between this weekly gathering and the larger journey many believers are preparing for now.

Leave room in this day for the khutbah, for extra salawat, and for Surah Al-Kahf. Let Friday not be crowded out by errands or screens. Sacred time becomes a mercy when we meet it with readiness.
