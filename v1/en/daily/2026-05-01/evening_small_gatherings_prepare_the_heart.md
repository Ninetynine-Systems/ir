One wisdom of Jumu'ah is that it keeps teaching us how to stop.

> "And whoever honors the symbols of Allah, indeed it is from the piety of hearts."
> - Surah Al-Hajj, 22:32

Every week, Allah asks us to turn away from distraction and gather for remembrance. That habit matters. A heart that never practices responding in small sacred moments will struggle when larger sacred moments arrive.

Hajj does not begin only with travel. It begins with learning to pause for Allah, to gather for Allah, to leave lesser things for Allah. Friday rehearses that in a merciful weekly rhythm.

If the day softened you, protect that softness tonight. Do not rush back into noise as if nothing happened.
