One of the clearest correctives in the Hajj passage is this:

> "Their meat will not reach Allah, nor will their blood, but what reaches Him is piety from you."
> - Surah Al-Hajj, 22:37

Allah keeps drawing the believer away from appearance and back toward inward truth. That applies to the whole season. The scale, expense, and visibility of an act do not guarantee its nearness to Allah.

What reaches Him is taqwa. That should calm the anxious and expose the performative. It means a quiet sincere heart may be nearer than a very visible effort.

Prepare for sacred days, but do not decorate the self in the process.
