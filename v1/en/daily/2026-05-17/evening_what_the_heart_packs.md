If the heart were a travel bag, what would be inside it tonight?

> "And whatever good you do, Allah knows it."
> - Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:197

That sentence comes in the same Hajj passage as the command to take provision. Allah knows every good thing, even the quiet ones that no one sees in the crowd.

This should comfort the unseen servant. The person caring for family at home. The person repenting privately. The pilgrim trying not to complain. The believer making dua for others without being known.

Allah knows what the heart packs.
