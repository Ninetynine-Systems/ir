As this stretch of days matures, one dua keeps returning like a thread through the whole arc.

> "Our Lord, accept [this] from us. Indeed, You are the Hearing, the Knowing."
> - Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:127

Acceptance is the anxiety of sincere people. It is what keeps worship from becoming self-congratulation. The closer a person gets to sacred things, the more he should feel his need for Allah's acceptance.

This is true for the pilgrim preparing for Madinah, and it is true for the believer still at home trying to prepare inwardly. Nearness to sacred places is not the final measure. Acceptance is.

Keep that dua near tonight.
