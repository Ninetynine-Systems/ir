As the expected first day of Hajj draws near, the heart needs less clutter.

> "And take provisions, but indeed, the best provision is taqwa."
> - Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:197

Taqwa often requires simplicity. Less argument. Less display. Less need to control every feeling. More surrender to what Allah has placed in front of you.

Today, remove one unnecessary weight from your heart. A grudge. A comparison. A complaint you keep rehearsing. Make room for answer.
