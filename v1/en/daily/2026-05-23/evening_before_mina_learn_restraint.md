Before the rites begin in the expected schedule, restraint is already a lesson.

> "...there is [to be for him] no obscenity, no disobedience, and no disputing during Hajj."
> - Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:197

The pilgrim does not only leave home. He leaves certain versions of himself behind: the version that must win every exchange, complain at every delay, and turn every discomfort into injury.

This lesson belongs to the one at home too. Sacred days are coming. Do not enter them with an untamed tongue and a crowded ego.

Ask Allah to make restraint feel like worship before the crowd tests it.
