Some hearts are now close to Madinah. Some are far away. The ayah still holds all of them.

> "So make hearts among the people incline toward them..."
> - Surah Ibrahim, 14:37

The leaning of the heart is itself a mercy. It means Allah has not left the servant indifferent.

If your body is far, do not think that the matter is over. Ask Allah to keep the inclination alive and pure. A heart still turning is a heart still being invited.
