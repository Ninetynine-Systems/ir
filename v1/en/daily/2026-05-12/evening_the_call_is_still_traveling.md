Fifteen days into this new stretch, the most striking thing is that the story is still moving.

> "And proclaim to the people the Hajj; they will come to you on foot and on every lean camel..."
> - Surah Al-Hajj, 22:27

Ibrahim's call is still traveling through the centuries. It reaches passports and prayer rugs, airport queues and quiet bedrooms, pilgrims preparing for the road and believers making dua from home.

That is part of the beauty of this season: the answer is larger than one form. Some answer with travel. Some answer with repentance. Some answer with longing and preparation until Allah opens the next step.

Do not underestimate a sincere beginning. The call is still moving, and Allah knows who is turning toward it.
