Beloved days should not make the believer frantic. They should make the believer sincere.

The Prophet ﷺ pointed to the special worth of righteous deeds in these days.
> - Sahih al-Bukhari, 969

The aim is not to perform religious intensity for others to see. The aim is to become truthful with Allah in the time He made honorable.

For some, that will mean extra worship. For others, it may mean finally leaving a sin, repairing a wrong, or becoming gentler in a house where patience has been thin.

Do not make these days only large in your calendar. Let them become large in your heart.
