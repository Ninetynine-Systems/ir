The tongue can be part of preparation too.

> "Indeed, Allah and His angels send blessings upon the Prophet..."
> - Surah Al-Ahzab, 33:56

A tongue busy with salawat becomes less available for roughness, complaint, and idle talk. That is already a gift.

If you want one small practice tonight, let it be this: send blessings and let your speech become gentler because of it.
