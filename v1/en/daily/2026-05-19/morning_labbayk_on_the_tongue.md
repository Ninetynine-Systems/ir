The talbiyah is the sound of answer.

> لَبَّيْكَ اللَّهُمَّ لَبَّيْكَ، لَبَّيْكَ لَا شَرِيكَ لَكَ لَبَّيْكَ، إِنَّ الْحَمْدَ وَالنِّعْمَةَ لَكَ وَالْمُلْكَ، لَا شَرِيكَ لَكَ
>
> "Here I am, O Allah, here I am. Here I am; You have no partner. Here I am. Indeed, all praise, blessing, and dominion belong to You. You have no partner."
> - Sahih al-Bukhari, 1549; Sahih Muslim, 1184

Even before saying these words as a rite, the believer can learn their meaning: I am answering You, my Lord. Not my ego. Not my distractions. Not the crowd.

Let this morning carry the spirit of answer.
