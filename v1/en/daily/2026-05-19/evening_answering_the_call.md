Ibrahim was told to proclaim, and the people were promised to come.

> "And proclaim to the people the Hajj..."
> - Surah Al-Hajj, 22:27

The talbiyah is one of the ways that answer becomes audible. It is tawhid in the language of movement: no partner for Allah, all praise for Allah, all dominion for Allah.

That is why the answer should not remain only on the lips. If the servant says "Labbayk" while still obeying every impulse, the word has not yet reached deep enough.

Let the call travel inward. Let the answer reform something.
