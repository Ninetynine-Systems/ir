Some of the most important work we do with Allah stays hidden for a long time.

> "And whoever fears Allah - He will make for him a way out and will provide for him from where he does not expect. And whoever relies upon Allah - then He is sufficient for him."
> - At-Talaq, 65:2-3

Not every preparation produces immediate results. Some of it looks like sincerity no one applauds. Some of it looks like a slow return to discipline. Some of it looks like longing that only Allah sees.

Tonight, do not underestimate hidden work. Allah can build futures out of things that still seem too small to name.
