The Quran speaks about Hajj with a scale our individual lives can barely hold.

> "And proclaim to the people the Hajj; they will come to you on foot and on every lean camel..."
> - Surah Al-Hajj, 22:27

The people will come. That is a promise tied to the call of Ibrahim. Across centuries, borders, and languages, Allah keeps bringing people from distant paths.

If you hear news of departures, arrivals, waiting rooms, and early movement in these days, remember what you are really witnessing: Allah sustaining an ancient answer.
