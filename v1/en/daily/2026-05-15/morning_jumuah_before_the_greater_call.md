This Jumu'ah comes while the greater gathering is drawing near.

> "O you who have believed, when [the adhan] is called for the prayer on the day of Jumu'ah, then proceed to the remembrance of Allah and leave trade."
> - Surah Al-Jumu'ah, 62:9

Every Friday trains the believer to answer. Leave what can wait. Turn toward remembrance. Listen before speaking. Make room for Surah Al-Kahf and extra salawat.

The ancient call of Hajj gathers nations, but the weekly call gathers the heart first. Do not miss today's smaller answer while thinking about the larger one.
