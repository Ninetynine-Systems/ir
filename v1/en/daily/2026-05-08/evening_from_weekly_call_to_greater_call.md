The faith keeps returning us to the same lesson in different scales.

> "And proclaim to the people the Hajj..."
> - Surah Al-Hajj, 22:27

There is a weekly call and there is a greater call. There is the adhan that gathers a town and the ancient proclamation that gathers nations. Both teach the servant to respond.

This is why sacred routines matter. They train the inner reflex. When Allah calls, the heart should want to move.

If today's Jumu'ah went well, thank Allah for the rehearsal.
