One of the most humbling duas in the Hajj story is this:

> "Our Lord, make us Muslims [in submission] to You and from our descendants a Muslim nation [in submission] to You. And show us our rites and accept our repentance."
> - Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:128

Even Ibrahim and Ismail asked to be shown the rites. They did not lean on closeness, effort, or status. They asked for guidance.

That is a needed reminder for anyone preparing for sacred work. Do not ask only for the chance to do it. Ask to do it rightly.
