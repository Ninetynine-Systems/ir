On the edge of the expected Hajj days, the dua of Ibrahim and Ismail feels especially close.

> "And show us our rites and accept our repentance."
> - Surah Al-Baqarah, 2:128

They asked to be shown. That is humility. Sacred action should never make a servant casual, rushed, or self-assured in the wrong way.

Ask Allah today for guided worship: not only to do the act, but to do it with the heart He loves.
