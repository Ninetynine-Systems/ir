There is mercy in being made small before Allah.

> "And whoever honors the symbols of Allah, indeed it is from the piety of hearts."
> - Surah Al-Hajj, 22:32

A pilgrim among multitudes learns that worship is not centered on the self. The schedule, the crowd, the heat, the shared space - all of it can loosen the illusion that life should move around one's own comfort.

That is not humiliation. It is training. The self becomes smaller so the symbols of Allah become greater in the heart.

If you are not in Mina today, you can still ask for that mercy: make me smaller before You, my Lord, and make Your signs greater to me.
