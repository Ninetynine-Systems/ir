If the expected calendar holds, today is the first day of Hajj movement, the day many pilgrims enter the rites and move toward Mina. Allah knows the final moon-sighting reality, and He knows every servant's intention.

> "And proclaim to the people the Hajj; they will come to you..."
> - Surah Al-Hajj, 22:27

Today, the answer becomes visible. White garments, quiet fear, tired bodies, raised voices, and hearts trying to say "Labbayk" truthfully.

Even from far away, answer with them in your measure. Renew tawhid. Make dua for acceptance. Let the day remind you that a believer's life is meant to be a response to Allah.
