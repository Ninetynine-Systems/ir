# Key Texts

## Source anchors

- Surah An-Nisa 4:17
- Surah Al-Furqan 25:70
- Surah At-Tahrim 66:8

Surah An-Nisa 4:17 teaches that repentance is accepted for those who do wrong and then turn back. Surah At-Tahrim 66:8 calls believers to a sincere repentance, showing that tawbah is not a casual phrase said while the heart plans to remain unchanged.

Surah Al-Furqan 25:70 adds a remarkable promise: for those who repent, believe, and do righteous deeds, Allah changes evil deeds into good ones. The verse does not make sin small. It makes Allah's mercy large while tying that mercy to real return and renewed obedience.

These texts keep the path balanced. Repentance is neither despairing self-hatred nor cheap confidence. It is serious about the wrong and hopeful about the Lord.
