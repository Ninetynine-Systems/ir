# House And Acceptance

## Source anchors

- Surah Al-Baqarah 2:125-129
- Surah Ibrahim 14:35-41
- Surah Al-Hajj 22:26-27

Ibrahim's story reaches the Sacred House with the same foundation: worship Allah alone. The Quran ties the House to purification, prayer, tawaf, and remembrance, not display.

When Ibrahim and Ismail raised the foundations of the House, their dua was not self-congratulation. They asked, "Our Lord, accept from us." That prayer reveals the heart of accepted work: effort does not make a servant independent of Allah. Even after building for Him, the servant still asks Him to accept.

Ibrahim also prayed for safety, provision, prayer, forgiveness, and righteous descendants. The road to Hajj still carries those meanings. It is a road of tawhid, surrender, and a heart that keeps asking Allah for acceptance.
