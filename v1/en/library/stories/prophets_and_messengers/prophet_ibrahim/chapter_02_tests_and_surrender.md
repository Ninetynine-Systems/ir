# Tests And Surrender

## Source anchors

- Surah Al-Anbiya 21:68-73
- Surah As-Saffat 37:83-113
- Surah Al-Baqarah 2:124

The Quran says Ibrahim was tested by his Lord and fulfilled what was asked of him. It also tells us how his people sought to burn him, and how Allah made the fire cool and safe for him.

Later, the Quran shows a different kind of test: a dream-command involving his son. The scene is kept restrained. A father speaks. A son submits. Both surrender. Then Allah ransoms him with a great sacrifice and leaves Ibrahim with praise among later generations.

The story teaches surrender without adding details Allah did not give. The believer does not need invented scenery for the lesson to be heavy. Ibrahim's greatness is already clear: when Allah commanded, he turned toward Allah.
