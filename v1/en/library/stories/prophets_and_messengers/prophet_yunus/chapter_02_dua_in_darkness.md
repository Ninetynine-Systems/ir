# Dua In Darkness

## Source anchors

- Surah Al-Anbiya 21:87-88
- Surah As-Saffat 37:143-144

In the darkness, Yunus called out with words that begin with tawhid and end with confession: there is no deity except You; glory be to You; I was among the wrongdoers.

The Quran then says Allah responded to him and saved him from grief. It also says that if he had not been among those who glorified Allah, he would have remained in the belly until the day people are resurrected.

This is why his dua is so beloved to distressed hearts. It does not argue. It does not bargain. It returns to Allah with worship, humility, and honest admission.
