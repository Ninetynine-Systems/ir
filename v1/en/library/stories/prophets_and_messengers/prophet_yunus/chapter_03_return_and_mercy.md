# Return And Mercy

## Source anchors

- Surah As-Saffat 37:145-148
- Surah Al-Qalam 68:48-50
- Surah Yunus 10:98

Allah cast Yunus onto the open shore while he was ill and caused a gourd plant to grow over him. Then he was sent to a people who believed, and Allah let them enjoy provision for a time.

Surah Al-Qalam tells the Prophet ﷺ to be patient and not be like the companion of the fish when he called out in distress. Then it mentions that Allah chose him and made him among the righteous.

The close of the story is mercy, not humiliation. Yunus is remembered as a messenger, a servant rescued by Allah, and a sign that repentance can begin even from the deepest darkness.
