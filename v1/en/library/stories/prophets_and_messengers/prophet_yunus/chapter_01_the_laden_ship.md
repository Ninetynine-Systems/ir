# The Laden Ship

## Source anchors

- Surah As-Saffat 37:139-142
- Surah Al-Anbiya 21:87

Yunus عليه السلام was among the messengers. The Quran tells us that he went away in anger, boarded a laden ship, and then was among those selected when lots were cast. The fish swallowed him while he was blameworthy.

The Quran does not ask us to fill the scene with unsourced details. It gives enough for the heart: a prophet in distress, a servant overtaken by the consequence of leaving before Allah permitted relief, and a darkness no human plan could open.

Even there, the story was not over. Allah's mercy can reach a servant where no one else can reach him.
