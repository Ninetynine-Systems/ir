# A Sign for Later People

## Source anchors

- Surah Al-Kahf 18:19-26

When the youth were awakened, they thought they had remained only a day or part of a day. Hunger sent one of them into the town with old coins and careful instructions to move gently and not expose them. That small act revealed how completely time had shifted around them.

The Quran shows that their discovery became a sign for other people, especially regarding Allah's promise and the reality of resurrection. The account then redirects the reader away from fruitless speculation about their exact number or the hidden details of their stay. Those matters became points of dispute, while the revealed lesson remained plain.

The story closes by teaching humility before what Allah has fully disclosed and what He has not. The people of the cave are remembered not so later generations can chase hidden facts, but so hearts can recognize that Allah protects faith, controls time, and will surely raise the dead.
