# When To Use It

## Source anchors

- Surah Al-Baqarah 2:127

This dua was spoken as Ibrahim and Ismail raised the foundations of the House. It teaches that righteous work is never treated as self-sufficient. Even after obedience, the servant still asks Allah to accept.

It is fitting after prayer, charity, service, study, Hajj preparation, or any act where the heart fears pride or incompleteness. The wording is brief, but it places the work back before Allah with humility.

No fixed repetition count is attached here. Recite it with need, sincerity, and hope.
